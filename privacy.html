Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Quotely (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Quotely mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address (if you sign in)
  • User identifier (Firebase UID)
  • Feedback messages and ratings
  • Submitted quotes or proverbs
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Non-identifiable device information
  • Interaction with advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Manage user submissions
  • Respond to feedback and support requests
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Enable rewarded advertisement features

3. Firebase Services

Quotely uses Firebase services provided by Google, including Firebase Authentication, Firestore (database), and optional Firebase Analytics. Firebase may collect and process data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Advertisements & Rewarded Videos

The App may display advertisements, including rewarded video ads. Ad providers may collect advertising identifiers, interaction data, and device-related information to serve relevant ads and provide submission rewards. Quotely does not control how third-party advertisers use collected data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information may be shared only with Firebase (Google services), advertising partners, or when required by law.

6. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide app services and comply with legal obligations. Users may request data deletion through the in-app feedback feature.

7. Children’s Privacy

Quotely is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the in-app feedback feature.
